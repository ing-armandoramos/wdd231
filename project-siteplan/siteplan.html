LearnFlow Site Plan

Name

LearnFlow

The site is designed to help students from middle school to university learn about self-learning methodologies and improve their study productivity. It provides educational content on study techniques, a Pomodoro timer to help with focus, and access to ambient music to enhance concentration.

Purpose

LearnFlow exposes proven methods for the purpose of learning while providing tools for helping students to concentrate and bring a state of flow. The site has:

  • Study Teachniques, information and techniques
  • Pomodoro Timer
  • Ambient Music

Scenarios

  • "How can I stay focused while studying without checking my phone every 5 minutes?"

    • Answer: The site helps this user with a Pomodoro timer and full-screen mode for minimal distractions.

  • "I want to improve how I take notes and prepare for exams, but where should I start?."

    • Answer: The site offers information on study methodologies like Cornell and Cambridge note-taking.

  • "Where can I listen to focus music without the distractions of Youtube?"

    • Answer: The site had music that helps the student focus with no commercials or ads.
